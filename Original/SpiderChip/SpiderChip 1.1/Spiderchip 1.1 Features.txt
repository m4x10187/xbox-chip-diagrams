Solder Free installation on Xbox V1.0 - V1.6
Plays every Import and Backup Game from any region.
Plays all homebrew (emulators, media player, mp3, divx.....)
The chip come pre-programmed with a modified cromwell bios. The bios allow to reflash the chip via a CD, or to lock/unlock the HD.
The external on/off switch (which is included) is optional. If the switch is not connected, the chip is always ON.
The chip come in a hard metal case, to protect the spider chip from any damage during shipping.
